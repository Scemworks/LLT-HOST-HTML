GEC Sreekrishnapuram

GEC Sreekrishnapuram is a premier engineering college located in Palakkad, Kerala. It offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, fostering innovation and excellence in technical education. Established in 1999, the college has grown to become one of the most sought-after engineering colleges in the state, with a strong focus on academic excellence, research, and industry collaboration.
